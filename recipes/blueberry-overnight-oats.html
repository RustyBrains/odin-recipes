Blueberry Overnight Oats

Description

Oats that are blueberried and then overnighted

Inredients

  • 1/3 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup milk of your choice
  • 1/4 cup vanilla greek yoghurt
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 2 tbsp nice biscuit, crushed
  • blueberries

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Leave overnight (up to 5 days)

Peach Pie Overnight Oats

Description

Oats that are peach pied and then overnighted.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup milk of your choice
  • 1/4 cup vanilla greek yoghurt
  • 1/2 tbsp nut butter
  • 1/2 tsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp honey
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 peach, diced

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Leave overnight (up to 5 days)
